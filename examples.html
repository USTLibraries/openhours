Setting Up the Calendar

There needs to be a dedicated calendar for hours. Simply add an event during the time span you wish (up to 11:59pm!) and for the event title enter "Open 7:30am - noon". Yes, it is redundant, but it allows you to do things like "8am - 1pm by appointment only" and stay up past midnight, "8am - 2am". However, you'll note that if you are open past midnight, the next day's hours will show.

There are no plans on changing the way items are placed into the calendar, it works as desired on our end. Feel free to tweak the code if you desire something a little different.
